R2L pipeline

A capture system built for robotics teams, not content libraries.

01

Capture

Record controlled and real-world motion through the right method for the skill.

02

Verify

Check quality, synchronization, visibility, task completion and usable variation.

03

Label

Add task, object, motion and context metadata that robotics teams can actually train on.

04

Structure

Package raw footage, annotations and capture conditions into clean dataset deliveries.

05

Deliver

Hand over robot-ready visual data for model training, evaluation and iteration.

The R2L attitude

Robots do not learn from perfect worlds only.

They need the half-open drawer, the awkward angle, the failed first grasp, the object slightly out of reach. We are interested in the parts of reality that make robots useful outside the lab.
